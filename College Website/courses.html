BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)

1. Duration of the Course :

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) Honours course shall cover a period of three academic years.

The medium of instruction shall be English / Hindi

Eligibility for Admission :

2.1 Candidate seeking admission to the First Year of the Degree of Bachelor of Business Administration will be required to have passed with a minimum of 45% marks, the intermediate Examination in Arts/Science/Commerce of a Board / University established or incorporated by law or any other Examination recognized by the University as equivalent there to.

2.2 The BBA course shall be mode on the basis of merit list prepared after the entrance test including written and viva-voce Examination to be candidate by the college.

TOTAL NO. OF SEATS: 60

The reservation of seats for the courses shall be as per rules of the state Government and T. M. Bhagalpur University for SC/ST and OBC. All candidate seeking admission to the courses shall pay full fees as prescribed in the regulations or modified from time to time under the provision of the regulations.

B.COM IN OFFICE MANAGEMENT AND SECRETARIAL PRACTICE(H)

B.com in Office Management & Secretarial Practices (Hons) is a UGC Sponsored 3 Years Bachelor Degree Course. The department has a placement cell for the arrangement of placement in industries, corporate sector, Multi National companies. A number of alumni are well placed in above fields. The college provides job training facilities during long vacation.

Office Management and secretarial Practices is UGC Sponsor 3 Yeas bachelor degree course. This course has been introduced in 1995 at the instant of UGC in order 10 Vocationalize degree level courses under new education policy of Govt. of India. Since than 23 batches have been completed successfully. Our alumni are well placed in the field of media/print media, corporate sector and multinationals companies.

This department has as well equipped and well furnished laboratory with modern office equipments. It also has all IT solutions to aware students of modern age and automate office work culture.

Eligibility for Admission:

A Candidate shall be admitted to Bachelor of Office Management & Secretarial Practice(H) provided she has passed 10+2 Examinations in any recognized Board and has offered at least one subject from the Faculty of Arts/Science/Commerce at her 10+2 level of study

Total Seats : 50

(Some seats reserved for candidates from other countries & states and decided by the University Administration.)
